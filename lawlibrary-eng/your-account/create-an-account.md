# Create an account

Creating an account helps you keep track of your research and return to important documents quickly.

## Create your account

1. Click **Log in** at the top of the site.
2. Click **Create an account**.
3. Enter your details and choose a secure password.
4. Click **Sign up** to create your account.

## If you cannot create an account

If you do not see a **Create account** option, account access may be managed differently for your site. Contact your site administrator or support team for access.
