# Get help and support

If you are blocked or unsure what to do next, you can contact the Legal Information Website support team for help.

## Contact support

1. Open your browser and go to /about/
2. Use the contact details on that page to reach the support team.

## Before you contact support

Share these details so support can help you faster:

- The page URL where the issue happened.
- What you were trying to do.
- Any error message shown on screen.
- The device and browser you are using.
