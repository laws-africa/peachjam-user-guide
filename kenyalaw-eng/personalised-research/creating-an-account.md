# Creating an account

1. Go to the **LawLibrary** homepage.
2. Click the **Sign up** button under My LawLibrary

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/kenyalaw--Create 3.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. This will open **the Create an account** page
4. Enter your:

* First name
* Last name
* Email address
* Password
* Confirm password

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/kenyalaw--create 4.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Click the **Sign up** button to submit the form.
6. A confirmation email will be sent to your email address.
7. Open the email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.
8. Return to LawLibrary and log in using your email and password.
